Welcome to mood's documentation!
================================

MOOD is a simple terminal-based text adventure game where multiple players can
explore a shared dungeon, interact with each other, and battle monsters.
The goal of the game is for players to work together, explore the dungeon, 
and defeat the various monsters and challenges they encounter.
